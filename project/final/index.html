About this App

This app was created by Dani Macedo, college student of UC Davis for an interactive media class led by professor Glenda Drew.

She made this interface off of inspiration from a scientific article from her psychology course about language and cognition. Knowing that people acquire different proficiencies in their second language depending on what age they began being exposed to it, she wanted to focus on making something that would help those who were less proficient and wanted to get better.

She designed this application to be a fun interactive learning tool for those that want to perfect their English in quick and direct ways. Her goal is to help second-language English speakers feel more confident in their English skills and more connected to the English-speaking community around them.
