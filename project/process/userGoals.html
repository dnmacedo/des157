User Goals

    The humans will understand the content of the project because it will be clear as soon as they open the interface. First by starting with the icon/logo for the application should be pretty clear on what the app does.

    Humans will understand the primary objectives through an easily navigable interface. It should be easy to get around and make sense upon first opening it.

    The users need a score counter to encourage their learning and make the games fun. The humans need to be able to go back and fortha and exit when they like. They need clarity in the form of straigtforward graphics and minimal instruction.

    My human audience would expect my project to be as a game and their experience level would be well experienced with apps that mine branches off of.
