Topic Choice

My choice of topic for the ISEA confrence is "peace within- finding inner peace." My project will be a service that may be a digital interface or physical, which will help people set goals for themselves and achieve those goals in every day life. My audience is primarily focused on individuals of any age that hope to minimize anxiety in their everyday life. I would like this project to not be something only people with a smartphone could use. It'll take time, but I am hoping that it will motivate and satisfy the needs of my particular audience. Perhaps I could cater it to a specific culture that would be unlikely candidates, such as indiginous groups of people. I haven't decided yet if I am going to be all-inclusive, or cater empathetically to one culture. But it seems my idea would be hard to cater outside the modern worlds. Still in progress!
