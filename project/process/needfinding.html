Needfinding

  • 1. The product needs to have clear subtitles.
  • 2. It needs to use pop ups as a last resort.
  • 3. The human needs to feel in control.
  • 4. Icons/pictures need to be clear in their meaning and functionality.
  • 5. Needs to be aesthetic, avoid aggressive colors.
  • 6. Navigation needs to feel natural.
  • 7. The human needs to be able to discriminate icons.
